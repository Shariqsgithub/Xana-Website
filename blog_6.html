Announcing an updated DICOM labeling experience, and new text highlighting capabilities

In the realm of medical imaging, where clinicians and drug developers rely on visual data for crucial decisions, an improved DICOM labeling experience is paramount. Today, I’m thrilled to share updates to our end-to-end DICOM labeling process within the Xana medical ecosystem.

Key Enhancements:
  • DICOM Labeling Experience: Our revamped DICOM labeling process streamlines tasks for users. You can now import DICOM files, create annotation tasks, view and label gold standards, and even participate in mobile labeling contests—all seamlessly integrated within Xana medical.
  • OHIF Viewer Integration: We’ve taken it a step further by integrating the OHIF Viewer, a world-class open-source viewer developed by MGH, into the Xana medical platform. This integration provides users with access to advanced visualization, measurement, and segmentation capabilities. Whether you’re dealing with CT scans, MRIs, or other complex modalities, the OHIF Viewer empowers labelers and reviewers. They can open a DICOM, apply segmentations, review existing ones, and effortlessly navigate through images—all from the desktop experience. Additionally, project managers can upload studies directly to Xana medical HIPAA and SOC2 Type II compliant platform, ensuring privacy and security.
  • Mobile Windowing Support: Our mobile DICOM viewer experience has also received an upgrade. Now, transitioning from manual desktop labeling to scalable mobile labeling is smoother than ever. Labelers can adjust window levels by simply dragging their finger across the screen. This dynamic viewer helps identify pathologies that might be invisible at certain window levels, allowing accurate evaluation and segmentation of DICOMs.
Xana medical continues to prioritize accuracy, security, and efficiency in medical data labeling. With the OHIF Viewer seamlessly integrated, SMEs can confidently interpret medical images while safeguarding sensitive data.
